# Mashed potatoes

## Ingredients
- 1 kg potatoes
- 50 g butter
- 100 ml milk
- Salt
- Pepper

## Instructions
- Peel and boil potatoes until soft.
- Drain the water and mash the potatoes.
- Add butter and milk, and mix until smooth.
- Season with salt and pepper to taste.
- - Optional: garnish with chopped chives before serving.
